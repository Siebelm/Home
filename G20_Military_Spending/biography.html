Biography



Michael Siebel joined Fors Marsh Group in 2015 as a researcher for the Department of Defense's (DoD) Office of People Analytics (OPA) Program. His primary responsibilities include designing, managing, and conducting data analysis on military personnel. This research requires deployment of techniques for machine learning, natural language processing, HTML-based visualization, and data warehousing on SQL Servers. His regular duties involve statistical programming in R, Python, and Stata; presenting results in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript; report writing; and oral presentation.

He is currently completing a M.S. in Data Science from George Washington University. He holds a M.A. in Political Science (focusing on survey methodology) from the University of Missouri-St. Louis and a graduate certificate in Survey Design and Data Analysis from George Washington University.
